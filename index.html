About

I am an Assistant Professor specializing in Computer Engineering at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, BITS Pilani – Hyderabad Campus.

My research interests lie in computer architecture, embedded systems, and human-centric technologies - building responsible, scalable computing systems.

Research and Teaching Philosophy: My goal is to equip students with skills to help them develop/apply various technologies to benefit the universe.
